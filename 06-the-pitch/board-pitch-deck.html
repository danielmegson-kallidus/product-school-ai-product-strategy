The Risks

What could break this

Trust / Failure
** The front-page failure is the agent auto-executing a destructive admin action - mass user deletion, mis-assignment of compliance training to the wrong population, payroll-adjacent data leaving the tenant. The contract: ≥92% accuracy on a 250-row gold set at the orchestration layer, ≥95% on the two action categories we auto-permit (data return and group operations). User deletion is never auto. Learning assignment always requires a human gate. Our red team found a tool-chain composition bypass; we validated it, shipped sequence-aware gating in H1 week 6, and added 28 adversarial gold-set rows at ≥98% accuracy. Hallucination ≥2% over 24 hours triggers automatic rollback.
Scale / Governance
** At 10x usage the watch item is COGS drift. If triage accuracy degrades the cascade tilts toward the frontier model and margin erodes. Mitigation is the per-seat fair-use ceiling (1,000 tasks per admin per month) with £0.15 overage. Governance posture is signed off: Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) complete, Data Protection Officer (DPO) sign-off at H1 week 4, EU AI Act limited-risk classification, EU customer data resident in EU infrastructure throughout. Scope is administrator AI only; learner-facing AI is a separate, future policy decision and we are explicit about that.
